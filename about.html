Our Founder

Susanto Liman

The story of MR.COFFEE begins with Susanto Liman, a passionate coffee enthusiast with a dream. Born into a family of tea merchants, Susanto's journey into the world of coffee was an unexpected one. During his college years, he discovered the transformative power of a well-crafted cup of coffee and the community that formed around it.

After graduating with a business degree, Susanto spent five years traveling to coffee-growing regions around the world—from the mountains of Colombia to the highlands of Ethiopia. He learned from local farmers about sustainable growing practices and the art of bean selection.

In 2015, Susanto returned home with a vision: to create a coffee shop that would serve as more than just a place to grab caffeine. He wanted to build a community hub where the coffee was exceptional, the atmosphere was welcoming, and every customer felt like they belonged.

With his savings and a small loan, Susanto opened the first MR.COFFEE in a tiny storefront with mismatched furniture and a single espresso machine. What the shop lacked in size, it made up for in character and quality. Word spread quickly about the exceptional coffee and the warm, creative atmosphere.

Today, as CEO of MR.COFFEE, Susanto remains hands-on in the business, often found chatting with customers or training new baristas. His commitment to quality, community, and creativity continues to be the guiding principles behind everything MR.COFFEE does.

Our Mission

At MR.COFFEE, our mission is to make all taste coffee every day. We believe that a great cup of coffee has the power to inspire, connect, and transform. We are committed to sourcing the finest beans from sustainable farms, crafting each drink with care and precision, and creating spaces where community can flourish.

We strive to make exceptional coffee accessible to everyone, educating our customers about the journey from bean to cup, and fostering a culture of appreciation for the craft of coffee making.

Our Vision

We envision a world where coffee is more than just a beverage—it's a catalyst for connection, creativity, and positive change. MR.COFFEE aims to be recognized globally as a leader in coffee quality and innovation, while maintaining the warm, personal touch of a neighborhood café.

We see our coffee shops as community hubs where ideas are born, friendships are formed, and stories are shared. Through sustainable practices and ethical sourcing, we work toward a future where the coffee industry uplifts everyone involved, from farmer to customer.

Our Story

2015

The Beginning

MR.COFFEE opened its doors in a small storefront with just three employees. Despite its humble beginnings, the shop quickly gained a reputation for exceptional coffee and a welcoming atmosphere.

2017

Growing Community

As our customer base grew, we expanded to a larger location. We introduced our first community events, including poetry readings and local artist showcases, cementing our role as a neighborhood creative hub.

2019

Direct Trade Partnerships

We established direct relationships with coffee farmers in Colombia, Ethiopia, and Indonesia, ensuring fair prices for growers and the highest quality beans for our customers.

2021

Sustainability Initiative

MR.COFFEE launched a comprehensive sustainability program, eliminating single-use plastics and implementing a composting system for coffee grounds and food waste.

2023

Digital Expansion

Recognizing the need to connect with our community beyond our physical walls, we launched our mobile app and expanded our online presence to share the MR.COFFEE experience with a wider audience.

Today

Looking Forward

While we've grown, our core values remain unchanged. We continue to focus on quality, community, and creating spaces where every cup tells a story and every customer feels at home.
